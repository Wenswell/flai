# Task Phase

- Classify scope as tiny, normal, or deep before expanding process.
- Tiny means clear, low-risk work touching one or two files. Do not create task docs.
- Normal means bounded multi-file work or medium uncertainty. Use a short plan and relevant verification.
- Deep means vague, architectural, migration, security, permission, public API, or repeatedly failing work.
- Summarize current task state, blockers, next action, and verification.
- Keep `status.md` short.
- Keep process history in `log.md`, not in `status.md`.
